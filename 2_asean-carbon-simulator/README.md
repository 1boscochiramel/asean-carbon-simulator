# üåè ASEAN Carbon Integration Simulator

An interactive tool for analyzing carbon pricing policy divergence across ASEAN's petroleum refining sector.

## Overview

Based on the research paper: **"Carbon Pricing Integration for ASEAN's Petroleum Refining Sector: Policy Divergence, Ownership Dynamics, and Regional Harmonization Pathways"** by Bosco Chiramel.

## Key Features

### Tab 1: Regional Overview
- Interactive dashboard showing 25 refineries across 5 countries
- Total capacity: 4.76 million barrels per day
- Ownership vs. Carbon Price visualization (inverse relationship discovery)

### Tab 2: Carbon Liability Calculator
- NPV calculation for carbon compliance costs (2025-2050)
- Adjustable parameters: carbon price, escalation rate, coverage %
- Government fiscal exposure calculation
- Regional comparison charts

### Tab 3: Policy Divergence Analysis
- Timeline spread visualization (11-year gap)
- Price spread analysis (4√ó differential)
- Carbon price trajectory projections to 2050
- Competitiveness impact analysis

### Tab 4: Refinery Analysis
- Facility-level risk assessment
- Age distribution analysis
- Stranded asset risk identification
- Fleet summary by country

### Tab 5: Integration Pathway (NEW)
- Three-phase roadmap (2025-2045)
- Interactive milestone timeline
- Price convergence simulation
- Scenario comparison (baseline vs delayed)

### Tab 6: Malaysia Decision Analyzer (NEW)
- Countdown to Q3 2025 deadline
- Commitment vs Delay scenario comparison
- Cost-benefit analysis ($185M vs $2-5B)
- Malaysia refinery position assessment

### Tab 7: Investment Analysis (NEW)
- $229M investment breakdown
- Funding source analysis
- 3:1 benefit-cost ratio calculator
- Technology cooperation opportunities
- Risk assessment matrix
